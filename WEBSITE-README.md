## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues:

**Q: How do I edit the text?**
A: Open .html files in Notepad/TextEdit, change text, save.

**Q: How do I add photos?**
A: Put photos in `pictures/` folder, then update HTML to point to them.

**Q: The contact form doesn't work?**
A: Forms need a backend service (see DEPLOYMENT-GUIDE.md for Formspree setup).

**Q: How do I change colors?**
A: Edit the `:root` section at the top of styles.css.

**Q: Site looks broken after editing?**
A: Check for typos. You may have deleted a `>` or `"` by accident.
