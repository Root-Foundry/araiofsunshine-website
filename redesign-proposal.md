# Website Redesign Proposal
## A Rai of Sunshine of North Florida Inc.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

**Primary Objectives:**
1. Make information easier to read and find
2. Improve mobile user experience
3. Increase donations and member signups
4. Better showcase services and impact
5. Enhance accessibility for all users

---

## 📊 Current Site Challenges

### Navigation
❌ **14+ menu items** - overwhelming for visitors  
❌ **Inconsistent formatting** - uppercase vs. sentence case  
❌ **No clear hierarchy** - hard to find information quickly

### Content Organization
❌ **Scattered information** - requires clicking through many pages  
❌ **Small text** - difficult to read, especially on mobile  
❌ **Important CTAs buried** - Donate button not prominent

### Mobile Experience
❌ **Not optimized** for phones and tablets  
❌ **Difficult navigation** on small screens  
❌ **Slow loading times**

---

## ✨ Proposed Solutions

### 1. Simplified Navigation
✅ **5 main menu items** instead of 14+  
✅ **Clear hierarchy** with logical sub-menus  
✅ **Prominent "DONATE NOW" button** always visible

```
Before: 14+ scattered links
After:  About | Services | Get Involved | Resources | Contact
```

### 2. Improved Homepage Layout

**NEW STRUCTURE:**

1. **Hero Section** - Clear message, beautiful imagery, immediate CTAs
2. **Mission Statement** - Easy to read, prominent placement
3. **Three Core Services** - Visual cards with icons
4. **Testimonial** - Social proof from community members
5. **Impact Stats** - Show your reach and results
6. **Get Involved CTAs** - Multiple ways to engage
7. **Newsletter Signup** - Stay connected

### 3. Better Visual Hierarchy

**Typography Improvements:**
- Larger, easier-to-read fonts
- Better line spacing for comfortable reading
- Clear headings that organize content
- 16-18px body text (current web standard)

**Color Enhancements:**
- Sunshine Gold (#FFD700) - Hope and optimism
- Sky Blue (#4A90E2) - Trust and stability
- Warm Orange (#FF8C42) - Energy and action
- High contrast for better readability

### 4. Mobile-First Design

✅ Responsive design that works on all devices  
✅ Touch-friendly buttons (easy to tap)  
✅ Simplified mobile menu  
✅ Fast loading on cellular connections  
✅ Easy access to Call and Donate on phones

### 5. Enhanced Accessibility

**Critical for your mission!**

✅ WCAG 2.1 AA compliant (web accessibility standard)  
✅ Screen reader friendly  
✅ Keyboard navigation  
✅ High contrast text  
✅ Alt text for all images  
✅ Adjustable font sizes

---

## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- 🟡 Sunshine Gold - Represents hope and positivity
- 🔵 Sky Blue - Represents trust and support
- 🟠 Warm Orange - Represents action and energy

**Supporting Colors:**
- ⚪ Clean white backgrounds
- 🟤 Warm cream accents for sections
- ⚫ Dark gray for readable text

### Typography

**Headings:** Montserrat (bold, modern, professional)  
**Body Text:** Open Sans (clean, readable, friendly)  

All fonts optimized for screen reading and accessibility.
