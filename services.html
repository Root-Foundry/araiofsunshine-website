Our Services

Empowering Families, Building Independence

At A Rai of Sunshine of North Florida Inc., we provide comprehensive support services designed to enhance the lives of individuals with disabilities and their families. Since 2008, we've been dedicated to creating pathways to independence, community connection, and personal growth.

Family Support & Resources

Personalized Guidance

We understand that every family's journey is unique. Our founder, Sunnie Washington, draws from her personal experience as a parent of a child with disabilities to provide compassionate, practical support that truly makes a difference.

What We Offer:
• One-on-one family consultations
• Resource identification and connection
• Navigation assistance for services and benefits
• Advocacy support for educational and healthcare needs
• Peer support networks with other families

Entrepreneurship Assistance

Creating Flexible Opportunities

Inspired by Sunnie's own journey to entrepreneurship, we help individuals with disabilities and their family members explore business ownership as a path to greater independence and flexibility.

Our Support Includes:
• Business idea development and planning
• Resource connections for funding and support
• Mentorship from successful entrepreneurs
• Networking opportunities within our community
• Guidance on balancing business goals with caregiving needs

"It was easier for me to manage the needs of my home and my son by being my own boss. Working for myself provided the flexibility I needed to be more involved in Kevin's care." — Sunnie Washington, Founder

Monthly Community Meetings

Building Connections That Matter

Our monthly gatherings are the heart of our community – a place where families come together to share experiences, learn from each other, and build lasting friendships.

Meeting Details:
When: Last Sunday of each month at 3:00 PM
Where: 850 N. Jefferson Street, Unit 805, Monticello, FL 32344
Who: All families and individuals affected by disability are welcome

What to Expect:
• Supportive community atmosphere
• Resource sharing and updates
• Guest speakers and educational topics
• Networking with other families
• Light refreshments and fellowship
