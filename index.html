Our Mission

A Rai of Sunshine of North Florida Inc. enhances the lives of individuals with disabilities in Jefferson County and surrounding areas by providing essential support and resources. We also offer assistance to both aspiring and existing entrepreneurs.

Our Amazing Sponsors

How We Help

Three core services supporting our community

Support for Families

Resources, connections, and community for families navigating disability challenges. Our monthly meetings provide a space to share experiences and access valuable information.

Learn More

Entrepreneurship

Guidance and resources for caregivers seeking flexible work through entrepreneurship. We understand the unique challenges faced by caregivers in traditional employment.

Learn More

Community Meetings

Monthly support group meetings for families with members who have disabilities. Connect with others, share resources, and build lasting friendships.
